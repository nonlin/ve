Family Law

Family problems can be stressful and trying. Call Victoria today to start working toward a solution to your family law matter. Victoria has experience dealing with child support issues, child custody, or conservatorships, and divorces.

Many times people are afraid to file for divorce, or to go to court over a parenting issue. Victoria is sensitive to her client's lives, their personal decisions, and the various family dynamics in every unique situation. Victoria is eager to meet with you and start discussing possible solutions to your family matter. Victoria can help provide instant feedback, and start coming up with the proper strategy in your case. Ever family law matter can be different, just like every family is different.

Victoria will aggressively start working on a solution for you, and prides herself in her accessibility and communication with clients.
