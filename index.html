About

Victoria Erfesoglou graduated "cum laude" from the University of Houston with Bachelors of Arts Degrees in Philosophy and Psychology. She received her Doctor of Jurisprudence from Golden Gate University's School of Law, in San Francisco, California. She was proud to deliver the Commencement Address for her law school graduating class. Victoria is licensed to practice law in the states of California and Texas. She has a certificate of proficiency from the Greek government for her abilities in the Greek language. Throughout law school she actively participated in various areas of the law. She was a judicial extern for a probate judge in the San Francisco Superior Court, and also interned at the San Francisco Public Defender's Office.

Few lawyers are as deeply committed to client-centered representation and especially to criminal defense, as Victoria. She regularly represents the indigent, and is a proud graduate from the nationally recognized organization Gideon's Promise, class of 2013. Gideon's Promise's core principles are rooted in the landmark Supreme Court decision in Gideon v. Wainwright Gideon v. Wainwright wherein the Court decided that poor people, who cannot afford an attorney are entitled to one. If you are interested in learning more about Gideon's Promise and/or this important Supreme Court decision, please refer to the links on the left.

She is a pleased participant of the few lawyers selected for the FACT [Future Appointed Counsel Training] Program created by the Harris County Public Defender's Office, and has worked locally with some of the most dedicated criminal defense lawyers. Victoria was also selected to participate in the Texas DNA Evidence Mixture Review Project with the help of the Texas Forensic Science Commission.

Victoria is proud to help offer a solution to your family law, criminal law, or immigration law matter. She is eager to discuss misdemeanors, felonies, divorces, child custody, child support, removal proceedings, appeals, petitions for non-disclosure, expunctions, grand jury proceedings, writs of habeas corpus, or related matters with you. Please contact Victoria today to arrange a consultation.

Criminal Law

Contact Victoria today to start working on a solution for your pending criminal charges. Navigating the criminal "justice" system can be difficult and frustrating. Victoria understands that dealing with the government is overwhelming. You don't have to be alone during this difficult time, and you shouldn't. Remember, you have the right to remain silent, and you should exercise it. But if you were arrested, or are facing criminal charges, contact Victoria for a free consultation.

Immigration Law

Victoria has personally observed the immigration consequences of even minor criminal charges. If you or a loved one are facing criminal charges, and are not a United States citizen, it is crucial that you consult an immigration attorney for the potential consequences of a guilty plea (even if the plea is for a deferred adjudication). Removal proceedings in immigration court are very stressful for families and require great attention to detail, and communication with the client, and family. Please contact Victoria to further discuss any potential immigration concerns.

Family Law

Family problems can be stressful and trying. Call Victoria today to start working toward a solution to your family law matter. Victoria has experience dealing with child support issues, child custody, or conservatorships, and divorces.
